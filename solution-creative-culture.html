Creative & Culture

Pushing cultural boundaries

ELM enables large-scale public art and immersive environments for museums and theme parks. Our technology supports advanced facade systems, complex canopies and scenic event productions that conventional methods struggle to deliver economically.

From government monuments to theme park scenography LFAM gives curators and creative directors forms that were previously considered impossible at programme speed.

Benefits

LFAM for cultural programmes

Geometry, narrative and public impact define the brief LFAM delivers without the cost and schedule penalties of bespoke conventional fabrication.

  • Monument-scale forms without mould economics
  • Immersive environments and scenic production
  • Complex canopies and advanced facade systems
  • Rapid iteration for creative director sign-off
  • Concrete and polymer routes under one partner
  • Government landmark and cultural district experience
