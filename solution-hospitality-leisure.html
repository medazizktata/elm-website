Hospitality & Leisure

The interior is the brand

In hospitality, walking through the door is the experience. Five-star properties in Dubai compete on design — and enormous pressure falls on whoever translates architect intent into built reality.

ELM removes that pressure: a custom lobby sculpture that would take weeks in conventional fabrication ships from our facility in a fraction of the time. Late-stage design changes are file updates, not new tooling.

Benefits

LFAM for Luxury Interiors

Recycled polymers with genuinely high finish quality — output that looks exactly like what was specified, at programme speed hospitality procurement demands.

  • High-finish polymer for five-star and premium retail
  • Design changes without mould or tooling delays
  • Iconic lobby pieces at architectural scale
  • End-of-life disassembly for interior refresh cycles
  • Recarlo Milan-grade finishing and panel systems
  • 72-hour RFQ response for qualified programmes

Applications

Where hospitality programmes use LFAM

From flagship retail to resort landscapes — polymer LFAM for environments where brand expression and schedule are non-negotiable.

  • Iconic lobby centerpieces and feature walls
  • Luxury retail flagship installations
  • F&B environments and branded dining spaces
  • Hotel corridor and suite fit-out elements
  • Resort landscape and poolside features
  • End-of-life disassembly for interior refresh cycles
