Architectural polymer without geometric limits

A multi-axis robotic arm extrudes polymer in precise layers — building facades, furniture, branded installations and lighting fixtures straight from a digital file. No mould, no formwork, no geometric constraint.

We primarily use recycled and bio-based materials including rPET-G, recycled polypropylene and PLA bioplastics. Build volume scales from a single statement piece to an entire floor fit-out.

BENEFITS

Why Polymer LFAM

Design changes are file updates — no new tooling, no programme delay. Near-zero off-cuts because the robot deposits exactly what's needed.

  • Zero geometric constraints — any form, any scale
  • Recycled and bio-based materials as standard
  • High-finish quality for luxury hospitality and retail
  • Design changes without tooling cost
  • End-of-life disassembly and material recovery
  • Caracol Heron AM 400 platform capability

Applications

Polymer LFAM excels where bespoke geometry, brand expression and programme speed define the brief.

  • Facade elements and cladding systems
  • Architectural features and canopies
  • Lighting fixtures and integrated media
  • Luxury retail and hospitality fit-outs
  • Lobby centerpieces and branded installations
  • Public art and immersive environments
  • Automotive, aerospace and marine mock-ups
  • Rapid prototyping for R&D programmes
