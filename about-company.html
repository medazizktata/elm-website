ELM MEDIA DESIGN

Industrial 3D Printing
at Architectural Scale

ELM Media Design was established to close a critical gap in the UAE market: commercial-scale large-format additive manufacturing (LFAM) delivered with the rigour of European engineering and the speed demanded by regional development programmes.

We are not a prototyping bureau. We partner with developers, contractors, government entities and hospitality brands to move architectural components from digital model through robotic fabrication to on-site installation.

Our capability spans robotic concrete 3D printing and polymer LFAM — technologies suited to facades, landscape elements, structural components and bespoke architectural features that conventional methods struggle to deliver at pace.
