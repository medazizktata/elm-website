How it works

Industrial LFAM, layer by layer

Industrial robotic systems extrude polymer or concrete layer by layer, building components without the moulds, formwork and tooling conventional fabrication cannot do without. It is genuinely different to anything else in this market.

Robotic precision, digital-first design and on-demand production, one platform logic across concrete, polymer and end-to-end architectural manufacturing.

Why we exist

Closing the UAE capability gap

We started ELM Media Design because the UAE was ready for this. Dubai has already mandated it. Yet there was no specialist company here doing it properly, at commercial grade, with the delivery discipline serious clients require.

That gap is what we are here to close.

Our ambition

LFAM as the standard choice

We want large-format additive manufacturing to become the standard choice for anyone building something ambitious in the UAE, not an exotic option that takes months of convincing to reach a tender list.

Every project we deliver should make the next client's decision easier. The technology speaks for itself once people see it. Our job is to show what it looks like in the real world.
