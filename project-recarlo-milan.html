Case study

Recarlo Fine Jewellery Boutique

Large architectural wall panels for the flagship Milan store designed as a story-telling surface carrying the brand's identity through form, texture and colour.

Delivered by Caracol AM (Italy) using the Heron AM 400 platform. High-finish luxury fabrication at a quality level directly applicable to UAE five-star hospitality and premium retail.

LOCATION
Milan, Italy
CLIENT
Recarlo Fine Jewellery
PLATFORM
Caracol Heron AM 400
TECHNOLOGY
Polymer LFAM
MATERIAL
Recycled PET-G + 20% glass fibre
PANEL SIZE
2m × 3.9m each
PRINT TIME
55 hours per panel
WEIGHT
160 kg per panel
FINISH
Milling and painting
VERTICAL
Hospitality & Leisure / Retail

Challenge

Create a bespoke architectural surface at luxury retail finish standard complex geometry, brand narrative and high visual impact without conventional mould tooling delays.

Outcome

Large-format panels produced via robotic polymer extrusion with post-process milling and paint finish. Proof that LFAM delivers five-star hospitality and premium retail quality at architectural scale.
