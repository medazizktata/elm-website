Why the UAE, why now

Policy mandates meet commercial LFAM

Dubai's 3D Printing Strategy 2030 mandates 25% of new buildings to incorporate additive manufacturing. Combined with Net Zero 2050, Dubai 2040 urban planning and ICV requirements, the market needs partners who deliver at scale not in a lab.

ELM exists to be that partner: authoritative, commercially disciplined, and technically credible.
