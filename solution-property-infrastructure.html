Property & Infrastructure

Built for Dubai 2030 compliance

Dubai's 3D Printing Strategy 2030 mandates 25% of new buildings to incorporate additive manufacturing. Dubai Municipality enforces 3D printing requirements for new buildings — an active, non-optional procurement rule. Developers and contractors who haven't started planning compliance will face tender evaluators asking for it by name.

ELM prints bespoke architectural features for show villas and flagship developments — custom concrete formworks faster and cheaper than traditional mould-making, landscape elements, community seating and full-service buildings on-site in days.
