Robotic concrete

Structural and decorative concrete at architectural scale

An industrial robotic arm extrudes concrete layer by layer — structural or decorative, depending on programme requirements. No formwork means complex geometry becomes buildable, often on-site, in days rather than months.

Our lime-based structural mortars actively absorb CO₂ during curing — a measurable sustainability advantage for ESG reporting and UAE Net Zero 2050 alignment.

Benefits

Why robotic concrete LFAM

Eliminates conventional formwork for bespoke elements. Reduces material waste by up to 60% compared to standard methods. Enables on-site production with portable systems.

  • No moulds or formwork for complex geometry
  • On-site printing — print where you build
  • Up to 60% material waste reduction
  • CO₂-absorbing lime-based mortars
  • Days to weeks vs 8–14 week conventional lead times
  • Proven in regulated environments (Bergamo Airport)

Applications

Where concrete LFAM delivers

Programmes where geometry, programme speed or sustainability metrics drive procurement decisions.

  • Full-service buildings printed on-site
  • Structural and decorative wall systems
  • Urban furniture and landscape elements
  • Community seating and utility shelters
  • Custom concrete formworks for iconic facades
  • Smart-city installations and public monuments
  • Airport and infrastructure service modules
