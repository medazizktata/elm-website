Mission

Built for Site, Not the Portfolio

To make large-format additive manufacturing a credible, commercially viable construction method in the UAE — enabling architects and developers to build faster, with less waste, and with forms that conventional methods cannot economically produce.

We measure success by components installed on site, not renders in a portfolio.

Vision

UAE Reference Partner for LFAM

To be the UAE's reference partner for industrial 3D printing in the built environment — the name programme directors specify when LFAM must work at scale, on schedule, and in compliance with national policy.

From Recarlo Milan to Bergamo Airport, our lineage proves LFAM in demanding real-world contexts. ELM brings that standard to Dubai.
