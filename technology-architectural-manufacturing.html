Architectural manufacturing

When the project doesn't fit a category

Some programmes demand more than a single technology route. Architectural Manufacturing is ELM's end-to-end service digital design, material selection, robotic fabrication and on-site installation under one commercial contract.

We move from approved digital model to site-ready components in days, providing UAE-ready solutions for landmark hospitality, luxury retail, government and cultural projects.

Capability

Design to site, one partner

We measure success by components installed on site not renders in a portfolio. Every engagement follows a disciplined scope → fabricate → deliver path.

  • Single point of accountability for LFAM programmes
  • Concrete, polymer or hybrid fabrication routes
  • Material specification and sustainability reporting
  • Finishing, logistics and installation coordination
  • ICV and Dubai 2030 compliance documentation
  • 72-hour RFQ response for qualified enquiries

Process

Four-step engagement

The same engagement model applies across all architectural manufacturing programmes clear expectations from first briefing to capability profile access.

  • Consultation 30-minute technical briefing and feasibility review
  • Facility tour production capability and platform demonstration
  • RFQ response substantive quotation within 72 hours
  • Capability profile documentation for procurement submissions
