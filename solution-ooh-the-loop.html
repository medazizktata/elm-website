THE LOOP

Kinetic media meets industrial robotics

THE LOOP is ELM's proprietary out-of-home media concept synchronized LED screens mounted on industrial 6-axis robotic arms. It combines ELM's heritage in urban advertising with advanced LFAM engineering.
