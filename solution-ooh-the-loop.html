Kinetic media meets industrial robotics

THE LOOP is ELM's proprietary out-of-home media concept — synchronized LED screens mounted on industrial 6-axis robotic arms. It combines ELM's heritage in urban advertising with advanced LFAM engineering.

Why Robotic OOH

Static billboards can't compete with kinetic, programmable media in premium locations. THE LOOP delivers motion, sync and spectacle — with the deployment speed event and media buyers require.

Synchronized LED screens on 6-axis industrial arms. Prime Dubai locations — Downtown, DIFC, Palm Jumeirah and City Walk. Portable exhibition units operational in 24 hours. Aligned with Dubai 2040 green corridor ambitions — built by the same LFAM team behind architectural programmes, with rapid deployment for activations and seasonal campaigns.
